Queen Swan

A queen who has chosen exile from her own reflection. Each piece in this series traces the instant where sovereignty and vulnerability meet at the same threshold — feathers held as armor, armor worn as skin.

Queen Swan — I

The First Feather

The moment before the transformation is visible to anyone but her. Structural silver, hand-finished, one of one.

Queen Swan — II

Crown Undone

Authority, worn loosely enough to be questioned. A piece that completes itself only on the body.

Queen Swan — III

Still Water

Silence, given a structure you can wear. The last piece of the series, and the quietest.
